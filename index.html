Philosophy of Physical Literacy

At The Birches Academy, we believe physical education extends far beyond traditional sports and games. Our comprehensive K-8 curriculum nurtures the whole child through movement, fostering physical literacy, social-emotional growth, and lifelong healthy habits. We empower students to discover joy in movement, build confidence, and develop essential life skills through carefully crafted, age-appropriate activities.

Developmental Approach

Age-appropriate progressions that meet students where they are

Inclusive Environment

Every student finds success and joy in movement

Life Skills Focus

Building character, teamwork, and resilience through physical activity

Physical Education Syllabus

Course Overview

Welcome to Physical Education at The Birches Academy! This comprehensive program is designed to develop physical literacy, promote healthy lifestyles, and foster social-emotional growth through movement and play.

Course Objectives

  • Develop fundamental movement skills and sport-specific techniques
  • Improve physical fitness and understand its importance for health
  • Demonstrate good sportsmanship and teamwork
  • Build confidence and self-esteem through physical achievement
  • Develop problem-solving and strategic thinking skills
  • Foster a lifelong appreciation for physical activity

Expectations & Policies

Dress Code

Students should wear appropriate athletic attire including:

  • Athletic shoes with non-marking soles
  • Comfortable clothing that allows for movement
  • Hair tied back if necessary
  • No jewelry that could cause injury

Participation

Active participation is essential for success in PE. Students are expected to:

  • Arrive on time and prepared
  • Give their best effort in all activities
  • Follow safety rules and instructions
  • Respect equipment, facilities, and others

Medical Excuses

Students with medical conditions or injuries should provide a doctor's note. Modified activities will be provided when possible to ensure continued participation.

Grading

Grades are based on:

  • Participation and Effort (40%)
  • Skill Development (30%)
  • Sportsmanship and Cooperation (20%)
  • Written Assignments and Assessments (10%)

Safety Guidelines

  • Always warm up before physical activity
  • Report any injuries immediately
  • Stay hydrated - bring a water bottle
  • Use equipment only as instructed
  • Help maintain a safe environment for all

Communication

Parents and students are encouraged to communicate any concerns or questions. Please feel free to contact the PE department at pe@birchesacademy.edu or schedule a meeting during office hours.

Make-Up Policy

Students who miss class due to absence can make up participation points by:

  • Completing a written assignment about the missed activity
  • Participating in an alternative physical activity with documentation
  • Attending an after-school PE make-up session

Special Events

Throughout the year, we host special events including:

  • Fall Field Day
  • Winter Games Week
  • Spring Track & Field Meet
  • End-of-Year Olympics

We look forward to an active and engaging year of physical education!
